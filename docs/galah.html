Galah

Eolophus roseicapilla

The Galah is about 35 cm (14 in) in length and it's weight is about 270–350 g (10–12 oz). It's back is pale silver to grey, a pale rump, a hot pink face and breast, and a light pink mobile crest. It has a bone-coloured beak and the bare skin of the eye-ring is caranculated. Galahs have grey legs.
