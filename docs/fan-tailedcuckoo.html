Fan-tailed Cuckoo

Cacomantis flabelliformis

The Fan-tailed Cuckoo is a slender cuckoo, the adults are easily identified by their yellow eye-ring (slightly greenish in young birds), the Fan-tailed Cuckoo is generally dark slate-grey back and wings become pale rufous below, with a boldly barred black and white undertail. Younger birds are less attractive and browner in colour.
