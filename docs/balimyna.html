Bali Myna

Leucopsar rothschildi

The Bali Myna is an almost plain white bird with a long, floppy crests and black tips on it's wings and it's tail. It has bare blue skin around the eyes, greyish legs and a yellow beak. Males and females look similar in appearance. The Bali Myna is one of the rarest, and most beautiful birds in the world.
