African Firefinch

Lagonosticta rubricata

The African Firefinch is a beautiful finch found in most countries that are south of the Saharan Desert all the way to South Africa It is 4.0-4.7 in (10.2-11.9 cm) long and 0.3-0.4 oz (8.6-11.7 g). It has a clutch size of 3-6, the chicks at birth are altricial and it's Least Concern.
