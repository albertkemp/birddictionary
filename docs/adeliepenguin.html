Adelie Penguin

Pygoscelis adeliae

The Adelie Penguin is a small penguin, the height is 40 to 70 cm and it weighs 3.5-6 kg. It lives in many islands on the Antarctic Coast and it is a very common penguin, it's conservation status is Least Concern. It mainly feeds on small fish, krill and squid. It is mainly black with a white belly, black facial feathers cover most of the orange beak and it has a slim white circle around it's eyes.
