Fairy Prion

Pachyptila turtur

The smallest prion, the Fairy Prion measures between 23 and 28 cm (9.1–11.0 in) long. It has blue-grey plumage on it's upperparts and white underneath. The Fairy Prion has a dark "M" it's underparts extending to it's wingtips, and they have a wedge-shaped tail with a dark tip. They have a blue bill and feet.
