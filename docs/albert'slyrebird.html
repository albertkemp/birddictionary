Albert's Lyrebird

Menura alberti

The Albert's Lyrebird is a ground-dwelling bird. Females can reach about 75 cm (30 in) in length and males reach 90 cm (35 in). Their wingspan is 76-79 cm (30-31 in) and they weigh about 930 g (33 oz). Their colour is chestnut brown and they have a rufous undertail, rump, and throat.
