Zigzag Heron

Zebrilus undulatus

The Zigzag Heron is a small, short-tailed, black-crested, cryptically coloured dark hero, typically ocurring in dense South American forests. The Zigzag Heron's head and neck are mottled grey with a black crest forming a prominent mane giving the bird a thickheaded appearance. They feed on fish, reptiles, amphibians, crustaceans, molluscs and aquatic insects.
