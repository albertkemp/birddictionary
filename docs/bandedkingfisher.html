Banded Kingfisher

Lacedo pulchella

The Banded Kingfisher is a robust, medium sized river kingfisher. It has Dark blackish upperparts with silvery-azure streak from it's mantle to it's rump. It has long, white neck-flash with rufous tips and it's throat is white becoming yellow -buff on the sides; the rest of the underparts mainly light yellow-buff (male) and orange (female).
