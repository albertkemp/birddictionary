Kakapo

Strigops habroptilus

The world's only flightless parrot, the Kakapo eats plants. They often eat fruit from the tips of high rimu branches, supplejack vines and orchard tubers dug out from the ground. Kakapos are a large parrot with an owl-like face and moss green mottled with yellow and black above. They have a grey bill with the legs and feet grey with pale soles.
