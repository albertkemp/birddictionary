Pacific Baza

Aviceda subcristata

A slender, medium sized bird is recognized as the Pacific Baza. It's appearance is similar than other species in it's family. It grows up to 35–46 centimetres (14–18 in) in length with a wingspan of 80–105 centimetres (31–41 in) and a tail length of 19–23 centimetres (7.5–9.1 in).
